Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Meridian, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: Email address and encrypted password when you create an account.
  • Research queries: The scientific questions you submit to generate research synthesis.
  • Generated content: AI-generated research synthesis, connected ideas, hypotheses, and knowledge graph data created from your queries.
  • Usage data: Session timestamps, query counts, and feature usage to improve our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Meridian research platform.
  • Generate personalized research synthesis using AI models.
  • Build and maintain your persistent knowledge graph across sessions.
  • Find connections between your past and current research through semantic analysis.
  • Send service-related communications (if applicable).

3. Third-Party Services

Meridian uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Google Gemini API: Your research queries are sent to Google's Gemini AI model for processing. Google's Terms of Service and privacy practices apply to this data.
  • Supabase: Your account data, research sessions, and knowledge graph are stored in a Supabase-hosted PostgreSQL database with row-level security.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored in a secure, encrypted database with access controls.
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms through Supabase Auth.
  • We implement rate limiting, input sanitization, and Content Security Policy headers to protect against attacks.
  • Research data is associated with your user account and not shared with other users.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Export all your research data at any time through Settings → Account → Export Data.
  • Delete: Delete all your sessions and research data through Settings → Account → Clear All Data.
  • Portability: Download your data in JSON format for use elsewhere.
  • Opt out: Disable knowledge graph building and cross-session connections in Settings → Privacy.

6. Data Retention

Your research data is retained for as long as your account exists. If you delete your account, all associated data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Meridian is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes through the application interface.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please reach out through the application's Settings → Account page.
